Search for Ligand Information for Particular Ligand Entity in RDCdb

In the "RDC Search" section, users can search the full-text content of the RDCdb database to identify relevant RDC entries by entering keywords such as RDC drug names, target names, ligand names, linker names, radionuclide names, chelator names, and other related terms.

To perform a search, users simply enter a keyword in the main search box and submit the query. The search results page will display comprehensive information on all RDC drugs directly associated with the search term, including the RDC name, drug development stage, drug type, ligand name, linker name, chelator name, radionuclide name, and detailed links to the corresponding target information.

In order to facilitate a more customized input query, the wild characters of "?" are also supported.

(1) If search "FAPI-04", find a single API entry which is named "FAPI-04";

(2)If search "FAPI", finds a series entries with Ligand names.

The search results for "FAPI-04" display the Ligand ID, name, synonyms, structure chemical structure, mechanism of action, external links, and other relevant details.

The "Related RDCs"section is about Each Radionuclide Conjugate Related to This Ligand, which displays theradionuclide drug conjugates (RDCs) associated with this Ligand. Clicking each button will redirect users to the corresponding detail page for that radionuclide drug conjugate (RDC).

The "Complete List of RDCs Associated with This Ligand" section systematically displays the RDCs bound to this ligand. The list presents different types of RDCs along with their structural component information. Users can browse the different RDCs as a whole or click the RDC information to quickly navigate to the corresponding RDC activity page.

3.1 By clicking the "RDC Info" button, the detailed information page for particular RDC will be displayed

Using [177Lu]Lu-TE-FAPI-04 as an example, the "General Information of This RDC" section displays the General information of [177Lu]Lu-TE-FAPI-04, including its ID, drug name, aliases, drug status, indications, chemical structure, mechanism of action, external links, and other relevant details. In addition, this section also presents General information related to [177Lu]Lu-TE-FAPI-04, including the cold compound name, ligand name, linker name, chelator name, radionuclide name, and radiochemistry information.

In the "Full List of Activity Data of This RDC" section, three types of activity data are provided: in vitro activity data, in vivo animal data, and human activity data. Under the each subsection, users can view information.
