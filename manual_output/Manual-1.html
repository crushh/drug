Search for Radionuclide-Drug Conjugate Information for Particular Drug Entity in RDCdb

In the “RDC Search” section, users can search the full-text content of the RDCdb database to identify relevant RDC entries by entering keywords such as RDC drug names, target names, ligand names, linker names, radionuclide names, chelator names, and other related terms.

To perform a search, users simply enter a keyword in the main search box and submit the query. The search results page will display comprehensive information on all RDC drugs directly associated with the search term, including the RDC name, drug development stage, drug type, ligand name, linker name, chelator name, radionuclide name, and detailed links to the corresponding target information.

In order to facilitate a more customized input query, the wild characters of “?” are also supported.

(1) If search “[68Ga]Ga-C1-FAP-2286”, find a single API entry which is named “[68Ga]Ga-C1-FAP-2286”;

  1. If search “FAP-2286, finds a series entries with RDC names.

The search results for “[68Ga]Ga-C1-FAP-2286” display the Radionuclide-drug conjugate ID, RDC name, drug status, drug type, cold compound name, linker name, ligand name, chelator name, radionuclide name, and target name.

The “RDC Information” button redirects users to the RDC drug detail page for [68Ga]Ga-C1-FAP-2286. The “Cold Compound Information” button redirects users to the detail page of the unlabeled RDC drug. The “Ligand Information” button redirects users to the detail page for FAP-2286. The “Linker Information” button redirects users to the detail page for the linker(S)-2-amino-3-(((S)-1-(ethylamino)-1-oxo-3-sulfopropan-2-yl)amino)-3-oxopropane-1-sulfonic acid. The “Chelator Information” button redirects users to the detail page for DOTA. The “Radionuclide Information” button redirects users to the detail page for Gallium-68. The“Target information” button redirects users to the detail page for FAP.

Using [68Ga]Ga-C1-FAP-2286 as an example, the “General Information of This RDC” section displays the General information of [68Ga]Ga-C1-FAP-2286, including RDC ID, drug name, synonyms, drug status, indications, chemical structure, mechanism of action, external links, and other relevant details. In addition, this section also presents General information related to [68Ga]Ga-C1-FAP-2286, including the cold compound name, ligand name, linker name, chelator name, radionuclide name, and radiochemistry information.

In the “Full List of Activity Data of This RDC” section, three types of activity data are provided: in vitro activity data, in vivo animal data, and human activity data.

Under the “Detailed Activity Data - Human Activity Data” subsection, users can view information on indications, patient characteristics, efficacy data, and related descriptions.

Under the “Animal Activity Data” subsection, imaging or ex vivo biodistribution data are presented, including animal models, cell models, metabolism and excretion information, tumor retention time, therapeutic efficacy, and visualized activity data. For key data, the T/B values are calculated and displayed in tabular format.

The “in vitro data” section presents the lipophilicity coefficient, relevant binding affinity data, and related descriptions.
